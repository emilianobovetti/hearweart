Hear We Art in Summary

Hear We Art is a charitable project enabling professional artists, designers and other creatives to deliever creative workshops to poor, deaf Sri Lankan children

Hear We Art has clearly defined aims, key among which are to:
  • Help the children acquire vocationally oriented skills and capabilities.
  • Boost the children’s confidence and self-esteem and at the same time bring joy, pride and sense of achievement.
  • Involve local creatives and teachers in the programme, to both promote the objective developing autonomy and help build links to the community which can help children transition to much more worthwhile work.

News & Activities

This year, we will be running four courses: Graphic Design, Photography, Wood Carving and Mural Art. The three main courses are aimed at giving students some work-related skills which will help them in find a much better place in the community, economically and socially, when they finish their studies
